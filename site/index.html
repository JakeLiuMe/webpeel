Frequently Asked Questions
What happens if I exceed my limits? ▼
Soft limits apply — your requests will slow down but won't stop completely. You can upgrade at any time or purchase additional credits.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes, absolutely. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel your subscription anytime from your dashboard.
Do you store the pages I fetch? ▼
No, we never store fetched content. We only log metadata (URL, timestamp, response time) for analytics and debugging.
Is there an enterprise plan? ▼
Yes! Contact us at support@webpeel.dev for custom pricing and dedicated infrastructure.
Can I self-host instead? ▼
Yes, WebPeel is open source under the MIT license. Install it with npm install -g webpeel and run your own instance.
